The Problem

Modified cars are hard to sell.
Impossible to trust.

Buyers can't verify what was installed, who did it, or whether performance claims are real. Sellers can't justify the premium. Shops get no lasting credit for quality work. There's no standard record — just "trust me."

The Solution

Every verification layer added to a build increases its trust — and its value at resale.

01
Owner Reported
Self-documented. A good start.
Baseline
02
Documentation Attached

Better
03

Trusted
04
Performed By Shop
Shop directly did the work. Maximum confidence.

01 — Your Garage

One home for every build

Document mods, maintenance, installs, and costs in a structured profile built for enthusiasts — not spreadsheets.

02 — Share Your Build

A link that does the talking

Every build gets a shareable URL. Drop it in a listing, bio, or auction and let the record speak for itself.

03 — Teams & Clubs

Find your crew

Teams and clubs maintain a linked roster of member build cards. Your community, documented and connected.

04 — Shop Verification

Prove the work was done right

Shops verify modifications directly on your build record. No more "trust me" — just proof.

About

Gaugd is built by a small team of car enthusiasts and software engineers. We've been on both sides of modified car deals — the buyer who had no way to verify the claims, and the seller who couldn't justify the price. We're building the tool we wish had existed.
